Making Mint Margaritas!

Learn how to make margaritas

Ingredients

  • Limes
  • Salt
  • Crushed Ice
  • Tequila Reposado

Instructions

  1. Grind the mint leaves.
  2. Add lemons' juice, salt and sugar to water, and mix nicely
  3. Blend the mixture with the ground mint leaves
  4. Top the blended mixture up with soda and add ice as per required
  5. Serve to your loved ones, and enjoy!
